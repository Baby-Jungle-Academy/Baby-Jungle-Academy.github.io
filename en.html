Academy Rules & Regulations

We are committed to providing a safe and respectful environment for all children

1No hitting under any circumstances
2No insulting or verbally abusing the child
3No sick children, or children with a fever or rash, will be admitted
4Children of determination / special needs are welcome, provided they do not cause harm to others
5Hygiene and cleanliness of the child, facility, and staff are strictly mandatory
6No celebration of non-Muslim holidays
7A complete change of clothes must be brought with the child daily
8Parents are not permitted inside the academy except during enrollment, which is available at any time of day
